This is the most important part. Show something real. Build live. 🎯

🔧 STAGE 2-4: PRACTICAL PROMPTS 5 min
LOAD

Give them something they can use tomorrow. Turn your talk into a toolkit.

PRODUCT THINKING
"Act as a product strategist. Suggest three ways this feature could increase retention."
DESIGN FLOWS
"Generate a complete UX flow including empty states, error states, and edge cases."
RESEARCH
"What are potential usability risks with this design?"
