Privacy Policy

What we keep. What we don't.

Effective: 2026-05-16

The short version

Cusp generates reflection rituals based on your birth date and the intention you write down. The text is processed in‑memory by an AI model and discarded. We never store your intentions, never train on them, never sell or share them, and never link them to your identity.

Who we are

Cusp is published by Joey Primiani ("we", "us"). Contact: hello@cusp.app. This policy describes how the Cusp iOS app handles your information.

What we collect

Only what is required to generate a ritual:

  • Your birth date (year, month, day). Used to compute your sun sign and is sent to our backend with each ritual request. We do not collect birth time or location.
  • The intention you set. A short text string you write describing what you're bringing in. Sent to our backend, forwarded to the AI, and discarded after the ritual is returned.
  • An anonymous device identifier generated on first launch. Used only to enforce the daily free‑tier limit (3 rituals/day). It is not tied to your name, email, Apple ID, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

We do not collect: your name, email, Apple ID, contacts, photos, location, advertising identifiers, or behavior analytics inside the app.

How your data is used

  • Your intention and birth date are sent over HTTPS to our proxy server (Cloudflare Worker), which adds the AI prompt and forwards the request to Anthropic's Claude API.
  • Anthropic returns a three‑step ritual; we return it to your device.
  • Neither our server nor Anthropic retains the submitted text. Our server does not log intention content. Anthropic processes the request and does not use the input to train models.
  • The anonymous device identifier and a daily counter are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV for up to 30 hours, solely to enforce rate limits. They are not associated with any other identifier.

On‑device data

The app stores the following on your device only, and never transmits them:

  • Your birth date
  • Your current intention
  • Today's ritual (so you can return to it without regenerating)
  • Ritual history (the rituals you've completed, up to 100)
  • Your daily streak counter
  • Your subscription status (Free or Pro)

Third parties

  • Anthropic, PBC processes the intention + astrological context to generate rituals. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare, Inc. hosts our proxy server. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Apple, Inc. handles subscription billing through the App Store. Apple Privacy Policy

No advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or marketing trackers are integrated.

In‑App Purchases

Cusp Pro ($7.99/month or $49.99/year with a 7‑day free trial) is processed by Apple. We receive only an anonymous receipt confirming entitlement. We do not see your payment method, name, or billing address.

Children

Cusp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we'll delete any anonymous identifier associated with that device on request.

Your rights

Because we do not store intentions or personally identifiable information, there is nothing identifying you to delete. You can:

  • Reset your anonymous device identifier at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.
  • Stop sending text to our server by simply not using the ritual feature.
  • Cancel a Cusp Pro subscription at any time through Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Residents of the EU, UK, and California: under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA respectively, we provide the same minimal‑data treatment described above to everyone. Contact us at hello@cusp.app with any data‑subject request.

About astrology and the rituals

Cusp is for reflection and intention‑setting. We do not predict the future. The rituals are AI‑generated based on your birth date, your stated intention, today's moon phase, and the current planetary day of the week. They are designed as prompts for thought and small actions — not as guarantees of outcomes.

Security

All network requests use HTTPS (TLS 1.3). Our backend secrets (Anthropic API key, Cloudflare credentials) are stored in encrypted environment variables and never exposed to the app binary.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how Cusp handles data, we will update this page and the effective date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.
