How it works

Three moves. Done.

Step one
Hold your mouse
Press and hold anywhere on any page for half a second. Works on images, links, text, and full pages. No clicking around.
Step two
Slide to a network
A radial pie menu blooms from your cursor. Slide toward the network you want — each direction maps to a different service.
Step three
Release & shared
Let go and Cortex shares instantly. A toast notification confirms with an undo option, then disappears after three seconds.
