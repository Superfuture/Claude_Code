# Required Image Assets

Place the following files in this directory:

## Hero Carousel
- `hero-1.jpg` — Editorial portrait (primary hero photo)
- `hero-2.jpg` — Speaking / on-stage photo
- `hero-3.jpg` — Media appearance / candid

Recommended: 2400×1600px minimum, JPG, face positioned center-left to stay clear of text overlay

## About Section
- `about.jpg` — Portrait (4:5 aspect ratio works best)
  Recommended: 1200×1500px minimum

## Video
- `video-poster.jpg` — Poster frame for speaking reel video
  Recommended: 1920×1080px (16:9)

## OG / Social
- `og-image.jpg` — Open Graph / social share image
  Required size: 1200×630px
