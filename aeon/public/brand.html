06 / VOICE

Speak in centuries.
Not in adjectives.

aeon's voice is confident, specific, and ancient. We do not pitch. We do not hype. We do not say "revolutionary." We say what we did and what we are trying to do, in language that would have been intelligible in 1900 and will still be intelligible in 2200.

01
Default to deep time.

If a sentence assumes a horizon shorter than a century, ask why. Frame impact against generations, not quarters.

02
Specifics beat superlatives.

"Three candidate cancer drugs in Phase II" is louder than "revolutionary breakthroughs." Numbers and verbs only.

03
Italic for soul. Roman for work.

Instrument Serif italic carries the mission. Barlow carries the substance. Never mix the registers.

04
White on void.

Restraint is the loudest move. When the room is shouting, our page is silent. Make people lean in.

05
Open by default.

Publish before you monetize. The default is to share — what we found, what we built, what we got wrong.

SAY THIS
  • "We compress decades of protein research into months."
  • "Three labs running the same agent. Outputs published Thursdays."
  • "Built for the next hundred years of research."
NOT THIS
  • "Revolutionary AI for tomorrow's problems."
  • "Cutting-edge solutions powered by next-gen tech."
  • "Unleashing the power of intelligence."
07 / MATERIALS

The surfaces
have weight.

aeon surfaces float in glass. The liquid-glass system gives every container a soft luminance — refracted light, faint edges. Never flat. Never solid colored panels.

LIQUID GLASS · STANDARD
04 px blur

Inset 1px highlight at top. Gradient mask edge. 1% white background. The whole UI floats on this surface.

background: rgba(255,255,255,0.01);
backdrop-filter: blur(4px);
box-shadow: inset 0 1px 1px rgba(255,255,255,0.1);
LIQUID GLASS · STRONG
50 px blur

Reserved for the single primary CTA on a page. Heavier obscuration earns a stronger highlight gradient (0.5 / 0.2 stops).

backdrop-filter: blur(50px);
shadow: 4px 4px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.05);
inset 0 1px 1px rgba(255,255,255,0.15);
VOID PLATE

Pure #000. Default canvas. Grain at 6% opacity to defeat banding.

AURORA GLOW

Radial aurora at 0.4 alpha, 20px blur. Only in moments of arrival.

HAIRLINE GRADIENT

Top-edge luminance gradient. Used for dividers and section separators.

09 / MOTION

Slow. Patient.
Like breath.

Ease-out 0.8s for entrances. Blur from 10px to 0. No bounces, no spring overshoots, no parallax dancing. The page arrives like dawn — gradually, then suddenly inevitable.

ease: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) · easeOut
duration: 700–1000ms
stagger: 80–150ms between items
10 / SOUND

Long form.
Low end. Sparse.

aeon sounds like a deep-space probe. Sustained drones in the sub-bass, faint metallic ring on accents, no melody hooks. Reference: Eno's Apollo, Hildur Guðnadóttir's textures.

BPM: none (durational)
Density: 1–3 elements at any moment
Texture: tape saturation, gentle reverb tails
