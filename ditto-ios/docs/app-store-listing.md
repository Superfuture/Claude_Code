## Description (4000 chars)
```
Ditto is an iMessage app that suggests three perfect replies to any text you receive — right inside the Messages app. No copy-paste. No switching apps.

When you don't know what to say, tap Ditto. It reads the message, generates three contextually-aware reply options, and lets you insert one with a single tap. Edit it if you want, then send.

PICK A TONE
Choose how you want to reply:
• Funny — playful, witty, never cringe
• Flirty — warm and charming, never crude
• Formal — clear, professional, polite
• Supportive — validating, present, calm

SOUNDS LIKE A REAL PERSON
Ditto writes the way people actually text. Lowercase by default. Contractions. Match the energy of the message you got. No corporate AI-speak. No em-dashes. No emojis unless the message you got uses them.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your messages are processed in-memory by an AI model and immediately forgotten. We don't store them. We don't sell them. We don't train on them. No account required. No advertising. The full privacy policy is one tap away.

WORKS RIGHT INSIDE iMESSAGE
Ditto is an iMessage app extension, not a separate keyboard or screenshot tool. Open Messages, tap the app drawer next to the input field, pick Ditto. That's it.

FREE TIER
5 reply suggestions per day, free, forever. Unlimited regenerations. All four tones. No ads.

DITTO PRO ($4.99/month or $29.99/year)
• Unlimited reply suggestions
• 7-day free trial
• Priority support
• Cancel anytime

Built with Claude Sonnet — the AI model widely regarded as one of the best for natural conversation. All requests go through our zero-retention proxy. The API key is never embedded in the app.

Made by an indie developer who got tired of staring at the chat. Three replies. Always ready.
```

## Reviewer Notes (paste into App Review Information)

```
Ditto is an iMessage app extension that uses AI to generate three reply suggestions for an incoming text message.

How to test:
1. Open Messages app on the simulator/device.
2. Open or create any conversation.
3. Tap the iMessage Apps icon (next to the input field, looks like an "A" or grid).
4. Find and tap "Ditto" in the app drawer.
5. Tap any of the 4 tone presets (Funny, Flirty, Formal, Supportive). The app calls our backend, which calls Anthropic's Claude API, and returns 3 reply suggestions.
6. Tap any suggestion to insert it into the iMessage draft.

The free tier allows 5 generations per day. Pro is unlimited via $4.99/month or $29.99/year subscription with a 7-day free trial.

Privacy: messages submitted are processed in-memory by the AI model and not stored. Privacy policy at https://superfuture.github.io/Claude_Code/ditto-privacy.html.

No account or login required.

AI disclosure: the description clearly states that suggestions are AI-generated by an LLM (Claude).
```
