## Description (≤4000)
Ditto suggests three perfect replies to any text you receive, right inside the Messages app. No copy and paste. No switching apps.

When you do not know what to say, tap Ditto. It reads the message, generates three reply options that fit the conversation, and lets you insert one with a single tap. Edit it if you want, then send.

PICK A TONE
Choose how you want to reply:
• Funny: playful, witty, never cringe
• Flirty: warm and charming, never crude
• Formal: clear, professional, polite
• Supportive: validating, present, calm

SOUNDS LIKE A REAL PERSON
Ditto writes the way people actually text. Lowercase by default. Contractions. It matches the energy of the message you got. No corporate AI voice, no emojis unless the message you got uses them.

WORKS RIGHT INSIDE iMESSAGE
Ditto is an iMessage app, not a separate keyboard or screenshot tool. Open Messages, tap the app drawer next to the input field, pick Ditto. That is it.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your messages are processed in the moment by an AI model and immediately forgotten. We do not store them, we do not sell them, and we do not train on them. No account required. No advertising.

FREE FOREVER
Five reply suggestions every day, free, with unlimited regenerations and all four tones. No ads.

DITTO PRO
Go unlimited for 4.99 per month or 29.99 per year, with a 7 day free trial. Unlimited suggestions, priority support, cancel anytime.

Built on Claude, one of the best AI models for natural conversation. Every request runs through a zero retention proxy, so your texts are never stored and the API key is never embedded in the app.

## Reviewer Notes (App Review Information)
Ditto is an iMessage app extension that uses AI to generate three reply suggestions for an incoming text.

How to test:
1. Open the Messages app on a device.
2. Open or create any conversation.
3. Tap the iMessage apps drawer next to the input field, find and tap Ditto.
4. Tap any of the four tone presets (Funny, Flirty, Formal, Supportive). The app calls our
   backend, which calls Anthropic's Claude API, and returns three reply suggestions.
5. Tap a suggestion to insert it into the draft.

Free tier is 5 generations per day. Pro is unlimited via a 4.99/month or 29.99/year subscription
