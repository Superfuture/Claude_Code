Common questions.

How does payment work?

You're charged on Stripe Checkout at the time of reservation. We confirm your slot within 24 hours and slot you in for the next available date. Receipts and invoices auto-generated.

What format does the ad take?

A native-feeling card with your logo, a short headline (max 60 chars), a 1-2 sentence pitch (max 140 chars), and one CTA. We review every ad before it goes live to keep the bar high.

Can I track conversions?

Yes — we hand you a UTM-tagged link, and every Monday we send an email with click-throughs, impression counts, and click-to-impression ratio.

What if the slot underperforms?

If a weekly or monthly slot delivers fewer than 70% of projected impressions, we credit the gap toward a future slot at no charge. We've never had to do this, but the offer stands.

Can I sponsor a specific category?

Yes. Custom category sponsorships (e.g. "Built with Cursor" leaderboard) are available on the monthly tier and via custom deals.
